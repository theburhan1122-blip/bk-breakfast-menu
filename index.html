Top 5 Breakfast Picks

#1

Bacon, Egg & Cheese Croissan'wich

This iconic sandwich features crispy bacon, fluffy eggs, and melted American cheese on a buttery croissant. It's the perfect balance of savory flavors and has remained a fan favorite for years. The flaky croissant texture sets it apart from typical fast-food breakfast sandwiches.

#2

Egg-Normous Burrito

For those with big appetites, this burrito delivers serious satisfaction. Packed with scrambled eggs, bacon, sausage, hash browns, cheese, and creamy spicy sauce wrapped in a warm flour tortilla, it's a complete meal in one hand-held package. At over 800 calories, it keeps you full until lunch.

#3

Sausage, Egg & Cheese Biscuit

A classic Southern-style breakfast option, this sandwich layers savory sausage patty, eggs, and cheese between a warm buttermilk biscuit. It's hearty, comforting, and pairs perfectly with hash browns and coffee. Many consider it the ultimate comfort breakfast.

#4

French Toast Sticks

These golden, crispy sticks dusted with cinnamon sugar and served with maple syrup are perfect for those who prefer something sweet in the morning. They're great as a side or a standalone treat, and kids absolutely love them. At just $2.29, they're budget-friendly too.

#5

Fully Loaded Croissan'wich

Can't decide between bacon, ham, or sausage? Get all three! This loaded Croissan'wich combines all your favorite breakfast meats with eggs and cheese on that signature buttery croissant. It's indulgent, filling, and worth every penny when you're extra hungry.
